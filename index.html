Bonus Tips

🚗 Wild Camping

Generally easy and tolerated in mountains, near lakes, and remote coastal areas. Avoid private property and restricted national park zones.

⛽ Gas Stations = Safe Spots

Turkish gas stations often have toilets, showers, WiFi, and mini markets. Perfect overnight stops when needed.

🧠 The Golden Rule

"That looks cool → let's turn → camp → continue." Don't overplan. Turkey rewards spontaneity.
